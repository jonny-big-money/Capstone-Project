NBA stands for National Basketball Association. It is a professional basketball league in North America that is considered the top basketball league in the world. History The NBA was established in 1946 The Basketball Association of America merged with the National Basketball League in 1949 to form the NBA The NBA is headquartered in Midtown Manhattan Teams The NBA has 30 teams, split between the Eastern and Western Conferences The Western Conference has the Northwest, Pacific, and Southwest Divisions The Eastern Conference has the Atlantic, Central, and Southeast Divisions Playoffs The top-ranked teams at the end of each season play in a playoff to determine the NBA champion The NBA champion is considered the world champion Other NBA leagues The NBA also includes the Women's National Basketball Association (WNBA), NBA G League, NBA 2K League, and the Basketball Africa League (BAL)
